THE HURL SCOUTS

☆

Austin Roller Derby Club

The Hurl Scouts are a female, amateur roller derby club who based in Austin who compete in the Texas Roller Derby league.

There's a lot more to derby than fishnets and picking out a tough name. This is a sport.

Game play consists of a series of short scrimmages (jams) in which both teams designate a jammer (who uniquely wears a star on the helmet) and four blockers to skate counter-clockwise around a track. The jammer scores points by lapping members of the opposing team. The teams attempt to hinder the opposing jammer while assisting their own jammer—in effect, playing both offense and defense simultaneously.

WANNA SKATE?

No prior skating or derby experience needed!
